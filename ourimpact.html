The Bertha Centre for Social Innovation and Entrepreneurship in partnership with Huruma Bantfu, a youth run strategic consultancy company, hosted an event for youth called 'Pathways to Information' in Khayelitsha at the Isivivana Centre. The event sought to connect young people to information, specifically information that can assist them in taking that next step in their learning, entrepreneurial and career development.



In 2021, Huruma Bantfu (Pty) Ltd in conjunction with The Bertha Centre for Social Innovation and Entrepreneurship hosted an event called Pathways To Information at Philippi Village. The event's focus was to connect youth to opportunities across 3 segments: Education, Employment and Entrepreneurship. Each segment featured guest speakers providing information and resources to the attending youth.
